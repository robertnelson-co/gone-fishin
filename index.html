Mr. Trash Wheel's River Cleanup Adventure

Mr. Trash Wheel has escaped from his home in the Baltimore river and is on a mission to clean up the most polluted rivers in the United States.

Use your up and down arrow keys to help Mr. Trash Wheel collect the garbage in the water!

Get as close to the trash as possible to gain points. If you miss more than 10 pieces of trash, you will have to try again another day.

If you would like to learn more about Mr. Trash Wheel and his environmentally conscious siblings, you can check out his home page!
